## Infrastructure

We support Linux, Berkeley Software Distribution (BSD), Mac, and Windows platforms. The following versions are required:

- Linux (2.6.18 or later) 
- BSD (NetBSD 8.x and FreeBSD 12.x) 
- OSX (10.7 Lion or later) 
- Windows 10
